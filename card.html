One of Motor's kin

Tabby cats are more vicious than Panthers and Tigers combined.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 3 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 15-20 years
  • Habitat: Household structures--anywhere they can interupt

With claws the size of fish hooks, the Tabby is able to snatch fish from lakes and streams weighing up to half a pound (226.8 grams).
